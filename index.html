UMSI 2022 · (352)-327-2499 · fangzy@umich.edu

Ziyang is a Front-End Developer, Game Designer and UX Designer

Hello, I'm Ziyang Fang. I am a game developer focusing on UI/UX designing and background and story writing. I have a wide range of interests in games of different genres. I especially enjoy games with historical backgrounds, such as King of Crusaders, European Universalis, Romance of Three Kingdowns, Total War, and Sid Meier's Civilization.

I love designing elegant and engaging product experiences for humans. I love working with creative individuals that can bring strong critique as well as laugh at the latest meme.

I thrive on the challenge of interaction and visual design efforts with product and development staff toward results that meet business and UX goals. I continually test tools and methods to find the most effective workflow. After years of studying and designing different UX projects, my days are lived in whiteboard sketches, wireframes, mockups, and of course, user research.

I admire design thinking, user testing, agile development, and learning. From UX designers to web developers, I can collaborate at every level.

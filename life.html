View My Life

Looking back to 2022, many changes have taken place in my life. I was in Florida preparing for my undergraduate graduation just over a year ago. In the blink of an eye, I was on my way home. Before getting on the plane, I spent a week in Seattle, visiting the Chihuly Garden and the Space Needle. Before I could say goodbye to this beautiful city, I landed in Shanghai and started my quarantine. During that time, I saw the turmoil before the lockdown of Shanghai and received the offer from UMSI. Many things made the 21 days of quarantine not so dull.

After the quarantine ended, I backed to Guangzhou. The half year in Guangzhou was the longest period I have spent at home since I went to college in 2017. It feels great to be with my family, and it is so good to see everyone healthy. During that time, I also had time to catch up with some old friends, and camping with mates was very impressive. The Gundam exhibition was also held in Guangzhou during that time, and it was fascinating to see the 1:1 Gundam.

Half a year flew by, and I had to return to Florida to start the move earlier than planned due to the lease on the apartment. I wondered why there was a watermelon next to the case when I packed the last box, so I also took this interesting scene.
